About Kerala
  • Location: Southwestern coast of India
  • Capital: Thiruvananthapuram
  • Language: Malayalam
  • Nicknamed: "God's Own Country"
  • Famous for: Backwaters, beaches, hill stations, and Ayurveda
  • Major Festivals: Onam, Vishu
  • Cuisine: Appam, Puttu, Kerala Sadya, Seafood
  • Tourist Spots: Munnar, Alleppey, Wayanad, Kovalam
  • Highest Literacy Rate in India
  • Traditional Art Forms: Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, Theyyam
  • Economy: Tourism, spices, coconut, fishing
  • Climate: Tropical monsoon climate
